The best TV Shows according to Giovanni

My 3 top TV Shows of all-time

The Walking Dead

It tells the story of a group of survivors living in a zombie apocalypse. The story takes place near Atlanta, Georgia and the Virginia wilderness. The group looks for a safe place to live, and these settlements are often destroyed by zombies hordes. The zombies are called "walkers" in the series.

Shameless

The series depicts the poor, dysfunctional family of Frank Gallagher, a neglectful substance dependent single father of six: Fiona, Phillip, Ian, Debbie, Carl, and Liam. As he spends his days getting high and drunk, his kids run through endless scams, affairs, and petty crimes throughout Chicago just to survive.

The Handmaid's Tale

The Handmaid's Tale is a book about a totalitarian state called Gilead which used to be the United States. Gilead runs on religious extremism. Most women in this futuristic society are infertile, except for a small group turned into handmaids. Handmaids are forced to conceive babies for privileged couples.
